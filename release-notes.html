    • Thanks to Michael Schmid, added the FHT#transform1D() and FHT#inverseTransform1D()methods (example Thanks to David Gauntt, fixed bugs that could cause the DicomDecoder#getFileInfo() method to hang when attempting to open non-DICOM files.
